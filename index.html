About Me

Hello my name is Anthony DiDonna. Currently I am a student at UCF going for a bachelors degree in Digital Media on the Game Design track. I have had a passion for gaming ever since I had picked up a Sega Genesis and played Altered Beast for the first time. I've always had an interest in trying to develop games. The ways you can interact with games are nearly limitless. I started this path beginning with programming and trying to learn all the different languages I could, even if it was just the basics of them. Some of those languages include C, C++, Javascript, and more. I also delved in the area of 3D modeling and I really enjoy that as well. Later on I plan to get into trying to make animations for my 3D models, and then try my hand at writing a story for a game eventually. I don't want to limit myself to one area of the process so I plan to continue learning everything there is about designing video games to make them fun interactive experiences that people won't want to forget.

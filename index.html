About Me

Hello my name is Anthony DiDonna. Currently I am a student at UCF going for a bachelors degree in Digital Media on the Game Design track. I've always had a passion for video games and that ended up in trying to develop games. I started with learning programming and trying to learn all the different languages I could, even if it was just the basics of them. I also delved in the area of 3D modeling and I really enjoy that as well. I don't want to limit myself to one area of the process so I plan to continue learning every aspect there is about designing and developing video games to make them fun interactive experiences that people won't want to forget.
